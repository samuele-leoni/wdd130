FAQ

Please make sure to take a look at this section before contacting us for a question, this can save both time and effort while also helping you have as much information you can get to enjoy this experience at its full potential.

Do you accept customers also without booking?

Unfortunately no, because we have to prepare our systems for the simulation in advance and it's done every morning for each day.

Is there any problem fitting glasses inside your headsets?

No, our headsets are made in a way that even glasses can fit under them.

Is there any problem regarding motion sickness and other vr related disorders?

Unfortunately it's impossible for to determine if one person will suffer from motion sickness, we always recommend to come prepared and in any case someone would experience those kind of disorders we'll stop the simulation for him/her.

Are there any documents needed in order to participate?

Yes, we need an ID to verify your age because for legal reasons we offer our experiences only to adults.
