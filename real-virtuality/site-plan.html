Overview

Purpose

Our goal is giving people the opportunity to live firsthand the experience of driving a race car, without the risks and costs connected to it. Thanks to the high technology and attention to details, our next generation simulators will offer people the chance to know exactly what a real pilot feels as he gets into his car and drives towards victory.

Audience

Our target audience is anyone from young adults age up or groups of young adults or business men and women who are searching for a fun way to race, together or alone. We are offering them a way to feel something different and intense through the accuracy and safety of the VR technology. Our customers will access our website more often using a PC brower.
